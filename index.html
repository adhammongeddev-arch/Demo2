Our Cafe Story

Founded in 1985, Golden Grain Cafe has been serving the community with quality coffees, pastries and light meals for over 35 years. Our baristas start work before dawn every day to ensure you get the freshest brew possible.

We use only the finest ingredients, sourcing locally whenever possible. Our coffee beans are ethically sourced, and we never use artificial flavors or preservatives.

What makes our cafe special is care - we prepare everything with attention to detail and passion for good food. Our team is dedicated to creating a warm, welcoming environment where everyone feels at home.

We're proud to be a community hub where friendships are formed, ideas are born, and memories are made over great coffee and food.
